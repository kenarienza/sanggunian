About the Sanggunian

We legislate for a progressive, orderly, and accountable Carmen

The Sangguniang Bayan ng Carmen is the municipality's legislative body, composed of the Vice Mayor as Presiding Officer and duly elected Sangguniang Bayan Members, together with the ex-officio members representing the Liga ng mga Barangay and the Sangguniang Kabataan Federation. Together, we enact ordinances and adopt resolutions that promote the general welfare of our constituents.

  • Open and transparent legislative sessions
  • Public access to ordinances and resolutions
  • Committee hearings open to concerned citizens
