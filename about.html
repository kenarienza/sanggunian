Our Mandate

The Legislative Body of the Municipality of Carmen

The Sangguniang Bayan ng Carmen is the local legislative body of the Municipality, established and empowered under Republic Act No. 7160, otherwise known as the Local Government Code of 1991. As the law-making arm of the municipal government, it enacts ordinances and adopts resolutions necessary for an efficient and effective local governance, and exercises such powers as are essential to the promotion of the general welfare of the municipality and its inhabitants.

The Sanggunian holds regular sessions to deliberate on proposed measures, conduct public hearings, and review the annual municipal budget, always guided by the principles of transparency, accountability, and participatory governance.

  • Enacts ordinances for public order, health, safety, and welfare
  • Adopts resolutions expressing the will of the municipality
  • Approves the Annual and Supplemental Budgets
  • Conducts public hearings on proposed tax measures
Mission & Vision

Guided by Service, Integrity, and the Rule of Law

Our Mission

To craft just, responsive, and forward-looking legislation that safeguards the rights and welfare of every constituent of Carmen, while upholding transparency and public participation in governance.

Our Vision

A Sangguniang Bayan recognized for principled leadership, sound public policy, and a legislative process that is open, inclusive, and accessible to every resident of Carmen, Surigao del Sur.

How Laws Are Made

The Legislative Process

From filing to enactment, every ordinance and resolution passes through a defined process.

1

Filing & First Reading

A proposed ordinance or resolution is filed and read for the first time on the floor.

2

Committee Hearing

Referred to the appropriate committee for study, public hearing, and deliberation.

3

Second & Third Reading

The measure is deliberated, amended if needed, and put to a vote of the Sanggunian.

4

Approval & Publication

Approved measures are signed by the Mayor and published before taking effect.
